+ Angels and Eagles
Angels and Eagles Art Gallery
Angels and Eagles Art Gallery features the art of Moore County North Carolina artist and resident "Mickeyangelo." Amazingly it is all created by hand and becomes more facinating the longer you look at it. Some of the pieces have been created over many years of time.
